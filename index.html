PhD, Institut Jean Nicod · École normale supérieure

Combining behavioural science and process improvement methodologies, my work investigates excessive sludge — unnecessary administrative complexity — through three intersecting questions:

  1. Why human cognition produces it.
  2. What it does to the people who experience it.
  3. How organisations and governments can detect and reduce it in practice.
